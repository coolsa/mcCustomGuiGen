Notes on process.
This is a bit of a breakdown of the math behind the whole process. It starts by taking the desired position for the item, in relation to the top-left of the image. The top left is the 0,0 position on the UV, which is the item's texture map. It starts at 0,0 and ends at 16,16 (aka the bottom right corner) The math behind this then takes that position relationship [X in, Y in, 0] , and then adds the position matrix [-8,8,8] to find the true center of the item. This center is used for the first portion of the actual thing... and stuff........ I really just mapped this out on a gimp file, whole bunch of notes on there...
